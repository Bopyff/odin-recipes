Adobo Recipes

A recipes for an adobo

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg of any meat of your choice
  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 1/2 cup of water
  • 3 Onions
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • Crushed pepper
  • 1/2 cup of soy Sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of vinegar
  • 4 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 bay leaves

Steps

  1. Mix everything together except the vinegar, water and bay leaves.
  2. Marinate it for atleast 30 minutes.
  3. Cook the meat in oil until you see a sear or golden brown.
  4. Add the marinate and wait for it to boil.
  5. Now add the water and boil it for 15 minutes while mixing it every 5 minutes.
  6. Now add the vinegar and don't mix it for 5 minutes.
  7. Now, most of the water should be evaporated and give it a taste.
  8. If the taste passed, then your adobo should be ready to serve.
  9. If it is'nt, keep boiling it until the water almost absorbed by the meat and evaporated.
